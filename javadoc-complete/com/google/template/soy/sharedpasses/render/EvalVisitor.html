NodeVisitor<ExprNode,SoyData>
public class EvalVisitor
extends AbstractReturningExprNodeVisitor<SoyData>

Visitor for evaluating the expression rooted at a given ExprNode.

Important: Do not use outside of Soy code (treat as superpackage-private).

AbstractReturningNodeVisitor.exec(N) may be called on any expression. The result of evaluating the expression (in the context of the data and env passed into the constructor) is returned as a SoyData object.
