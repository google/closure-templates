    NodeVisitor<ExprNode,SoyValue>

    public class EvalVisitor
extends AbstractReturningExprNodeVisitor<SoyValue>
    Visitor for evaluating the expression rooted at a given ExprNode.

    Important: Do not use outside of Soy code (treat as superpackage-private).

    AbstractReturningNodeVisitor.exec(N) may be called on any expression. The result of evaluating the expression (in the context of the data and env passed into the constructor) is returned as a SoyValue object.
